About the Training

This advanced training programme provides participants with a structured understanding of modern omics technologies and their integration in biomedical research and clinical applications.

The programme focuses on conceptual foundations, applied workflows, and real-world interpretation of genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and multi-omics data, without requiring advanced programming experience.

Programme Overview

Day Focus Content
Day 1
1 April		 Opening & Genomics Opening session, introduction to NGS technologies, genomics workflows, quality control, variant analysis concepts, and interpretation strategies.
Day 2
2 April		 Transcriptomics & Proteomics RNA-seq experimental design, differential expression interpretation, proteomics data structure, and differential protein analysis.
Day 3
3 April		 Multi-Omics Integration & Closing Principles of multi-omics integration, pathway-level interpretation, integrated case studies, and closing session with certificates.
