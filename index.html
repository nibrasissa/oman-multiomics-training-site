Advanced Multi-Omics Training Programme

A three-day intensive programme covering genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and multi-omics integration.

Register Interest Programme details

About the programme

This professional training programme is designed for students, researchers, and healthcare professionals seeking practical and conceptual understanding of modern multi-omics data analysis.

Three-day programme

Day Focus Key activities
Day 1
1 April		 Opening & Genomics
  • Opening session and orientation
  • NGS data types and workflows
  • Quality control and preprocessing
  • Variant analysis concepts
Day 2
2 April		 Transcriptomics & Proteomics
  • RNA-seq experimental design
  • Differential expression analysis
  • Proteomics data interpretation
Day 3
3 April		 Multi-omics Integration & Closing
  • Multi-omics integration strategies
  • Case studies
  • Closing and certificates
